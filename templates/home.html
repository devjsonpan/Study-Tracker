Study Session

Start a live timer to log your studying down to the second. Assign a course, topic, and personal notes to keep your sessions organized.

Notes

Every note you write during a study session is saved here. Browse, edit, or delete them whenever you need to revisit what you've covered.

Homework Tasks

Stay on top of your assignments by logging tasks with due dates and descriptions. Completed tasks can be marked off, and overdue ones are flagged automatically.

Events

Plan ahead by adding events with a start time, end time, location, and description. Perfect for study groups, exams, or any scheduled commitment.

Calendar

See all your homework deadlines and events laid out in a monthly calendar. Click on any item for a detailed summary.

Break Time

Track your breaks alongside your study sessions to get a clearer picture of how you're spending your time and staying balanced.

Study Summary

Dive into your personal analytics with charts broken down by course and daily trends. Join or create a Study Group to securely compare your stats with friends!
